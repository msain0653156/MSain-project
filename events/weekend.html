Weekend Events at Casita Sedona

Escape to the serene beauty of Sedona's canyons and immerse yourself in unforgettable weekend events. Whether you're looking to relax, explore, or enjoy some local culture, we have something for everyone!

Friday: Welcome to the Canyon

  • 5:00 PM - Canyon Sunset Viewing
    Kick off your weekend with a guided sunset tour through the Sedona Canyon. Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views as the canyon transforms with the setting sun.
    Location: Casita Sedona Terrace
  • 7:00 PM - Fireside BBQ & Stargazing
    After the sunset, join us for a relaxing fireside BBQ featuring local meats and vegetables. Enjoy a delicious meal as the clear desert sky opens up for an unforgettable stargazing experience.
    Location: Casita Sedona Backyard

Saturday: Adventure & Exploration

  • 8:00 AM - Morning Yoga & Meditation
    Begin your day with a peaceful yoga session led by a certified instructor. Stretch and breathe in the fresh canyon air as you set your intentions for the weekend.
    Location: Casita Sedona Garden
  • 10:00 AM - Guided Hiking Tour
    Explore the beauty of Sedona’s famous red rock formations with a local guide. Learn about the geology, history, and native plants as you hike the scenic trails.
    Location: Meet at Casita Sedona Entrance
  • 1:00 PM - Picnic Lunch at Oak Creek
    Enjoy a serene picnic lunch by the famous Oak Creek, known for its crystal-clear waters and tranquil surroundings. Relax and unwind in the stunning natural setting.
    Location: Oak Creek Picnic Area
  • 4:00 PM - Sedona Art Walk
    Explore the vibrant art scene in Sedona with a self-guided tour through local galleries. Many galleries offer live art demonstrations, giving you a chance to meet the artists and see their work up close.
    Location: Uptown Sedona
  • 7:00 PM - Sedona Wine Tasting & Dinner
    Experience the local flavors of Sedona with a wine-tasting session at one of the best wineries in the area. Enjoy paired appetizers and a gourmet dinner featuring locally-sourced ingredients.
    Location: Vineyards in West Sedona

Sunday: Relax & Unwind

  • 9:00 AM - Breakfast & Local Farmers Market Visit
    Start your day with a delicious breakfast featuring fresh, local ingredients. Then, take a short trip to the Sedona Farmers Market for locally grown produce, handcrafted goods, and more.
    Location: Casita Sedona Dining Room & Sedona Farmers Market
  • 11:00 AM - Desert Meditation & Sound Healing
    Reconnect with nature and yourself with a guided desert meditation session. Enjoy the peaceful sounds of the canyon while a sound healer guides you into deep relaxation.
    Location: Sedona Canyon Retreat Center
  • 1:00 PM - Farewell Lunch & Canyon Reflections
    Before you leave, enjoy a final lunch at Casita Sedona, complete with refreshing drinks and an open-air setting. Share your reflections of the weekend and plan your next visit!
    Location: Casita Sedona Dining Area
